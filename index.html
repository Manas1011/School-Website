about us

PG GARODIA SCHOOL (ICSE)

The school, Smt. P. G. Garodia English High School was established way back in 1969 and this became a reality due to the untiring efforts taken by Late Smt. Parmeshwaridevi. The school started off with seven students in a make shift room. Today it has blossomed into a very successful institution with more than 2000 pupils with classes right from playschool to standard X.



beyond academics

Library
Places where knowledge is obtained

Library forms an integral part of the school. Our library has been designed to cater to the needs of our students. Separate cubicles have been created to facilitate group study and reference work. A wide range of books and computer facilities have been added. Students are expected to do the project work in all the subjects and our library provides all the necessary requisites for the same.



Sports
Physical Fitness Matter

The school provides for a number of activities in the sporting curriculum to all pupils. We train our students in Basket-ball, Hand-ball, Table-tennis, Foot-ball, Chess, Carrom, Badminton etc. and we have commendable success in all the mentioned games as our pupils take part in a number of inter-school, district level, state level and national level matches which puts them on the apex. Apart from this, gymnastics and yoga is also taught to our students. There are training camps for the above mentioned activities.



ART
Art Matters too

Our children excel in this sphere too and their creativity comes to light. The children take part in various inter-school competition and win laurels for themselves and the school.

Mission STatement

  • OUR Mision towards future

    We seek to make our students • Value humanitarianism. • Comprehend the rich diversity of mankind. • Help foster civic mindedness • Inculcate national and cultural identity. • Promote healthy sportsmanship. • Conscientious global citizens. • Attain, acquire, aspire and achieve the set goals. • Develop into resilient, adaptable, compassionate human beings. • Active and diligent learners who contribute to the peace in the world.

  • Vision

    A Global society with intellectually, viable and capable young creative minds, in healthy bodies with ethical spirits, to contribute wisdom, compassion and leadership. An academic foundation that encompasses cultural, social, aesthetic, historical and physical understanding that prepares students to become innovative, principled and insightful leaders to make the world a better place to live in.
